My Capstone is a Short Film: A Play By Capri Gehred-O’Connell

My Capstone is a Short Film: A Play by Capri Gehred-O’Connell is a presentation on how I made a short film during my final year of my Master’s program. Or maybe it’s a little bit more. Maybe it’s an exploration into how to show up for myself, my work and the people around me. Maybe it’s a durational exercise in straightening my posture. Maybe it’s an ode to women who are soft but not malleable. Wait, sorry, can we go back and erase all those “maybes?” That is what it is! THAT IS WHAT IT IS!!!
